User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: September 19, 2025

Our game, "Click the Button Game", is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not create user accounts or store any personal information about you on our servers.

All game-related data, such as your score, is stored locally on your device within your web browser's storage.

How to Delete Your Data

To delete all data associated with the game, you can clear your browser's cache and site data. This action will reset the game and remove any stored information from your device.

Please refer to your browser's help section for specific instructions on how to clear cache and site data.
